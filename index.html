Windows 12

Hangman

The Windows 12 Concept website showcases a concept for the Windows 12 operating system. It's currently under development, so some features may not function properly. Visitors can explore the different sections to get a sneak peek of what the future of Windows could look like. Built using Bootstrap and jQuery, it demonstrates the designer's creativity and web development skills.

The website is a simple implementation of the classic game of Hangman. Players are presented with a blank word and have a limited number of guesses to correctly guess the word by selecting letters. The game ends when the player either correctly guesses the word or runs out of guesses. The website is designed to provide a fun and engaging way for players to test their word-guessing skills.
