Hangman

The website is a simple implementation of the classic game of Hangman. Players are presented with a blank word and have a limited number of guesses to correctly guess the word by selecting letters. The game ends when the player either correctly guesses the word or runs out of guesses. The website is designed to provide a fun and engaging way for players to test their word-guessing skills.
